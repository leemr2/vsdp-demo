## RESPONSIVE DESIGN NOTES

### Mobile Optimization (Critical - Board Members on iPad)
- Hero animation: Simplified version, smaller viewport
- Partnership cards: Stack vertically instead of horizontal layout
- Interactive timeline: Vertical scroll instead of horizontal
- Form: Condensed fields, larger tap targets
- Chat widget: Full-screen overlay on mobile instead of corner popup

### Tablet (iPad) Optimization
- Maintain visual richness of desktop version
- Touch-friendly interaction targets (minimum 44px)
- Horizontal scroll sections work well on iPad
- Video/animation: Ensure playback on iOS Safari

### Desktop
- Full interactive experience with animations
- Hover states for additional information
- Side-by-side comparisons utilize wide screen space

## CONTENT TONE & MESSAGING PRINCIPLES

### Tone Guidelines:
- **Confident but not arrogant:** "We've built something valuable" not "We're the only solution"
- **Technical but accessible:** Explain complex concepts, don't assume knowledge
- **Partnership-oriented:** "Let's build together" not "Buy our product"
- **Forward-looking:** Focus on future possibilities, not just current state
- **Evidence-based:** Cite real technologies (KAIST lenses), real companies (Meta CTRL-Labs)

### Avoid:
- Overselling vaporware: Be honest about what's built vs. roadmap
- Medical jargon without explanation: Define ERG, VEP, EOG clearly
- Defensive comparisons: Don't trash competitors, highlight VSDP strengths
- Pressure tactics: No false urgency or "limited spots available"

### Emphasize:
- Complementary capabilities: "You build hardware, we provide intelligence layer"
- Shared risk/reward: "Co-development means we succeed together"
- Strategic timing: "Platform inflection point—position now or license later"
- Real proof points: "KAIST already demonstrated wireless OLED contact lenses"
